About Ice Hockey Live

Bringing professional live coverage to grassroots and lower league ice hockey across the United Kingdom.

Our Story

Ice Hockey Live was founded with a simple goal: to give lower league ice hockey the professional coverage it deserves.


While top-tier leagues often receive extensive media exposure, many clubs, players and supporters in lower divisions have historically had little or no access to live broadcasts. We created Ice Hockey Live to change that.


By combining modern streaming technology, professional production standards and a passion for the sport, we provide clubs and supporters with reliable, high-quality coverage of games that would otherwise go unseen.

What We Do

Ice Hockey Live delivers live and on-demand coverage of ice hockey events throughout the UK. Our broadcasts are designed to offer viewers a professional viewing experience, including high-definition video, multi-camera production, live commentary and pay-per-view access where required.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help grow the sport by making ice hockey more accessible to fans, families and communities. Whether supporters are unable to travel, living overseas, or simply watching from home, Ice Hockey Live makes it possible to stay connected to the teams they love.
